# Changelog

## 1.0.0 — 2026-05-15

Initial public release.

- `SKILL.md` with 8 sub-commands (`status`, `setup`, `push`, `ipa`, `aab`, `release`, `fix-rejection`, `checklist`)
- Framework support: Capacitor, Expo, Flutter
- 5 helper scripts (TypeScript / Bun)
- Reference docs:
  - `REFERENCE.md` — framework differences, Fastfile templates, gotchas
  - `REJECTIONS.md` — recipes per Apple guideline (2.1, 2.3.8, 2.3.10, 4.0, 5.1.1) and Play Console issues
  - `MANUAL_FORMS.md` — Age Rating, App Privacy, Data Safety walkthroughs
- Auto-triggers on mentions of Play Store, App Store Connect, fastlane, TestFlight, gradle-play-publisher, or pasted rejection emails
